Apartment Bike Trainer Set Up

As much as I love biking in the snow... at night... while it's 2℉, sometimes that's just not the best option. I personally invested in an indoor bike trainer that I can actually hook up my own beat up road bike to.

It helps me get some extra miles in on days I'm busy or the conditions don't warrant, well, me risking my life for a bike ride. Cycling indoors can be tough with family and roommates though. Cost, noise, cooling and the the obscene amount of sweat the human body can produce often pose as common obstacles when it comes to indoor cycling.

In the video above, you'll find a complete overview of my indoor set up (a.k.a 'The Pain Cave') addressing all these issues.
